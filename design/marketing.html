I was the sole designer and developer for the marketing department. I was in charge of designing and developing all the landing pages, newsletters, emails, and sales decks.



Webcast Collateral

The marketing department launched a webcast campaign to help capture leads. This was a challenging project in a number of ways. I was given this project on my first day of work; My turn- around time for design and development was 3 days; and I was doing this all on the Pardot platform, one that I had never used before. Although this was challenging, it proved to myself how much I could accomplish in such a short amount of time. Turned out to be pretty fun :)








Email

Emails were a first for me. The challenge for me was the many constraints email design that I didn't know email had. All the CSS needed to be inline. Everything had to be in tables. In addition to being cross-browser compatible, the design also had to work with email clients that often stripped images and rendered text differently. This was also all done manually, because it was a requirement that the emails were sent through Pardot rather than through a service like MailChimp.

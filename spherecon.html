SphereCon


Responsive, Branding, LESS, CSS

Overview


I completed an exercise for Mesosphere. Scenario: Mesosphere is throwing it's first official conference, SphereCon 2015, and I was asked to take 3 days to design and develop a single page website for the event.



Goal


Create a vision for the look-and-feel of the event that evokes the right emotion and would also drive people to register.



Approach


I first approached this challenge by researching other tech conferences to give me a some inspiration. Then, I started to outline the different sections and take note of what's important to the page. I wanted my page to look cool and futuristic, but also welcoming.


This posed a challenge, because these characteristics are often contradictory. I accomplished the cool and futuristic characteristics by going with a minimalistic layout, choice of colors, and bold type. Initially, I had an icosahedron sphere as the hero background. Although this iteration captured the futuristic feeling that I sought, I felt that it lacked warm appeal. So, instead, I added a couple pictures of happy conference attendees to help site visitors better envision themselves at the conference.

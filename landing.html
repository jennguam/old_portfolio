Landing Page for Gaming Companies


Sketch, Wireframing, Web Development, HTML, CSS

Overview


This project was to be a marketing landing page that specifically targeted gaming companies for lead generation. It never made it to production because of budget cuts.


Role


I was the sole designer and developer for the marketing department. I was in charge of designing and developing all the landing pages, newsletters, and sales decks.


Goal


The goal of this landing page is to be the first impression of the company targeted especially for gaming companies, and what the product can do for them.


Challenge


I was approached to design and develop this landing page. I was presented with a 30+ slide Keynote file and was told to translate that to the landing page. The challenge was to shape the large amount of data and convey what the product has to offer into just a few value propositions.

Quick Mockup

Third Wave Coffee is a project between me and Gabe. It is meant to be a coffee bean review site. This is really useful to us, because we’re traveling the country trying different roasters.

There doesn’t exist something where we can take notes of flavor profiles, or look back at all the coffees we’ve tried. He is coding the back end whilst I design the front end. This is a mock-up to guide me while I transfer the design into code.

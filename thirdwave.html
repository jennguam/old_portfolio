Third Wave Coffee


Sketch, Illustrator, Photoshop CS6




Overview


This is a current side project between Gabe Grazier G'Sell and I. He is doing the back-end, I'm designing, and we're both going to work together to do the front-end. Third Wave is meant to be a coffee bean review site. This is born out of a need that both of us have for when we travel and try to find the best coffee in a new city. This need became very apparent when we were on our road trip and wound up in a new city every couple of days.


My goal for Third Wave is to create a coffee bean review app that is useful, fun, and intuitive. Right now it is still in it's early stages, and I want to have something in hand as soon as I can so I can test and iterate for improvement.


Third Wave will have 4 basic features

  • Discover New Beans

  • Share Coffee Experiences

  • Record Tasting Notes

  • Rate Coffee Beans

Measuring Success


Since Third Wave is still in its infancy, my milestones are:

  • High Fidelity Prototype with limited functionality

  • Completing a working app

  • App Store Submission/Acceptance

  • First Download

I would consider it to be a great success if I got more than 25 people to download it, but first I want to focus on first making the product!

Challenges


  • Balancing the social dynamics in the app

  • Moderating coffee roasters

  • Learning How to Code in Swift to make the app

  • Content Propagation

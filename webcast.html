Webcast Landing Pages


Sketch, Illustrator, Photoshop CS6

Unfortunately, I can't show much process in this one. The turn-around time for landing page and email template was 4 days for design and development on a platform I had never used before--Pardot. So, it was a bit of a mess, but I think it worked out.

Webcast Landing Page 1

Goals: Made sure user knew what the webcast was about, who the speaker was, when it was, and how to register

Webcast Landing Page 2

Webcast Invite Email

In my design, my goal was for the recipients to first, easily see when the webcast was and second, easily reserve their spot. If the recipient wanted to know more about what the webcast was about, then they could keep reading and find out. The point was to really make sure that they know where the "Save my Seat" was.
